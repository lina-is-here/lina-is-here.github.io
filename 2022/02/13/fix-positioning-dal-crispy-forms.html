Feb 13, 2022

Django-autocomplete-light library provides a great way to implement select widget with autocomplete feature in Django forms. I used it, and I am very happy with the result. But my form was still pretty ugly and the obvious and popular solution is to fix it with django-crispy-forms library. I love it! It’s easily configurable in the form class, and the only thing in the template to render the form is {% crispy form %} . Nice and clean!
